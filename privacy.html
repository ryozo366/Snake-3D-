🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Snake 3D ("we", "us", "our"), accessible at snake3d.online. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Snake 3D is a browser-based game. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage: Game settings, high scores, unlocked skins, and daily scores are stored locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.
  • Bug Reports: If you voluntarily submit a bug report, we collect the description you provide, your game mode, score, browser type, and screen resolution. Reports are submitted via Formspree.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may collect cookies and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

Snake 3D itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services integrated into our site may use cookies:

  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing behavior. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To process bug reports and fix issues
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Bug Reports: Submitted via Formspree (see Formspree's Privacy Policy)
  • Advertising: Google AdSense may process data as described in their privacy policy

Children's Privacy

Snake 3D is a casual game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through a bug report, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all locally stored game data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings
  • Contact us to request deletion of any bug report data

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our About & Contact page.
