🐍 About Snake 3D

What is Snake 3D?

Snake 3D is a free online 3D snake game you can play directly in your browser — no download required. Built with Three.js, it brings the classic snake game into a colorful 3D world with multiple game modes, maps, skins, power-ups, and upgrades.

Whether you want a quick casual round or a competitive challenge, Snake 3D has something for everyone.

How to Play

Control your snake and eat food to grow longer. Avoid hitting walls and your own tail!

Controls

  • ↑↓←→ or WASD — Move the snake
  • C — Toggle Orbit Camera
  • P — Toggle Retro / Pixel Mode
  • N — Toggle Night Mode
  • M — Open Maps
  • K — Open Skins
  • R — Retry / Restart
  • Esc — Close menus

Game Modes

🟢 Classic

The original snake experience. Eat, grow, survive.

👑 Snake Royale

Compete against AI snakes in a battle royale arena.

🧊 Cube Mode

Play on the surface of a 3D cube — snake wraps around edges.

♾️ Endless

No walls, no limits. The arena wraps around so you can play forever.

⏱️ Speed Run

Score as many points as possible in 60 seconds!

Features

  • Multiple Maps: Grass, Desert, Snow, Lava, Beach, Space, and more
  • Skins: Customize your snake's appearance
  • Upgrades: Earn coins and upgrade your snake's abilities
  • Power-Ups: Collect special items during gameplay
  • Night Mode: Toggle a beautiful night-time atmosphere
  • Daily High Scores: Track your best runs each day
  • Replay: Watch your last game back
  • Share Score: Share your achievements with friends
